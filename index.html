Summer

Summer,

I wanted to write you this letter because sometimes words are hard to say out loud, but I want you to know exactly how much you mean to me.

The very thought of u would fill me with joy and happiness. I would always look forward to the next time I could see that beautiful face or hear ur sweet voice. It doesnt matter what we do when we hang out or what we talk about.

I feel like I can tell u anything because it feels like there is nothing to fear or worry about because i know u care for me. There is this desire to be as kind a person as i am when im with u. I just want to improve myself as best as I can in every way because I dont want to offer u anything less.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for your kindness, your patience, and your love. I cherish every memory we have made and I cannot wait to make a million more.

I love u so much and i feel loved by u in a way that seems that the world would be a much darker place if i didnt have u.

Forever yours,

Alex
